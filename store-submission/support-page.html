Immigration Helper Support

Need help with Immigration Helper? Contact support at admin@immigrationhelper.org.

What To Include

Please describe the screen you were using, what you expected to happen, and what happened instead. Do not send immigration case numbers, passport numbers, financial information, or sensitive legal details.

About The App

Immigration Helper is independent and is not affiliated with USCIS, DHS, or any government agency. The app provides checklist, reminder, and general information tools only. It does not provide legal advice or determine eligibility for immigration benefits.
