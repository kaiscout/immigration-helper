Immigration Helper Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 8, 2026

Immigration Helper is an independent checklist, reminder, and official-resource app. It is not affiliated with USCIS, DHS, or any government agency. It provides general information only and does not provide legal advice.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app can save checklist progress, dates, language preference, onboarding status, and reminder-related choices locally on your device. This information is used to show your progress, help you organize reminders, and let the in-app helper summarize or update your checklist when you ask it to.

The developer does not operate a user account system and does not receive this local checklist data from the app.

Notifications

If you choose to set reminders, the app asks for notification permission and schedules local notifications on your device. You can deny notification permission or clear scheduled reminders in the app.

In-App Helper

The app includes a helper screen. In this first release, the helper uses built-in guidance and local checklist progress without sending your questions to a developer-operated server.

Do not enter sensitive personal information, immigration case numbers, alien registration numbers, passport numbers, financial information, or private legal facts into the helper.

Helper responses are for general information only. They are not legal advice and do not determine eligibility for any immigration benefit.

External Links

The app links to official resources such as USCIS pages and legal-help resources. When you open those links, they may open in your browser or another app, and the external website's privacy policy applies.

Data Collection By The Developer

The app does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, or third-party sign-in. The developer does not sell user data.

If you contact the developer for support by email or another external method, you may choose to provide contact information and support details. That information is used only to respond to your request.

Data Sharing

The developer does not share locally stored checklist data with third parties.

Data Retention And Deletion

Checklist and reminder data stored locally remains on your device until you delete the app, clear app data, or clear reminders where available. Support messages sent outside the app may be retained as needed to respond to support requests and maintain business records.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13.

Legal And Safety Notice

Immigration rules and filing requirements can change. Always verify information with USCIS or a qualified attorney or DOJ-accredited representative before filing, paying fees, traveling, or making immigration decisions.
